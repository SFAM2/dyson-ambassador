// About

THE PROGRAM

Dyson is one of the most inventive technology brands in the world, recognized for its design, engineering, and high-performance products. Since its founding in 1993 in the United Kingdom, the brand has expanded globally and built a strong reputation for premium consumer innovation.

This ambassador program is designed for creators who want to showcase products naturally, share authentic opinions, and collaborate on high-quality branded content. Participants gain exclusive opportunities while helping present the Dyson ecosystem in a clean, credible, and aspirational way.

PARTICIPATION CONDITIONS

Help us maintain a premium brand experience. These guidelines ensure your content stays on-brand while giving you creative freedom.

✓
Showcase the product clearly — highlight real problems Dyson products solve in authentic, everyday scenarios.
✓
Keep it exclusive — avoid showing competing products or unrelated visible branding in your content.
✓
Feature Dyson prominently — ensure Dyson products appear in at least 50% of your content duration.
✓
Stay authentic — keep your style natural and aligned with your personality while maintaining premium quality.
✓
